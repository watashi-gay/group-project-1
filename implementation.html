Football

Football, or soccer, is a globally popular sport where two teams of eleven players aim to score by getting the ball into the opposing team's goal. It’s known for its strategic plays, intense teamwork, and skillful footwork.

Basketball

Basketball is a fast-paced sport played by two teams of five players, where points are scored by shooting a ball through the opponent’s hoop. It’s celebrated for its dynamic action, dribbling skills, and slam dunks.

Volleyball

Volleyball is played by two teams of six players, separated by a net. Teams score by grounding the ball on the opponent's court, emphasizing quick reflexes, teamwork, and powerful spikes.
